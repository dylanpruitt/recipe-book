Peanut Butter Cookies

A quick and simple recipe for peanut butter cookies. Takes about 20-30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Directions

Mix sugars, peanut butter, butter, and egg in large bowl. Stir in remaining ingredients.

Heat oven to 375 Â°F.

Shape dough into small balls, roll into sugar, and place on ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten in crisscross pattern with fork.

Bake about 10 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from heat and cool.
