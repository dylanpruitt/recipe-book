Dorian's Mac and Cheese

Probably my favorite recipe for mac and cheese. Takes about 15-25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lb pasta
  • 1 1/2 oz. cheese (without anti-caking agents)
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • pepper, to taste

Directions

In sauce pot, combine pasta, salt, and 1 1/2 cups water.

Place pot on oven and heat on high.

Stir pasta occasionally. When water boils, start tracking the pasta cook time.

In a bowl, combine 1 tsp. cornstarch and 2 tbsp. cold water. Stir to form slurry mix.

On a cutting board, cut cheese into small pieces. Sprinkle 1 tsp. cornstarch over cheese, and mix until cheese is completely covered in cornstarch.

If pasta water is evaporating too quickly, reduce heat and add 1/2 cup hot water to pot.

This is the trickiest part about this recipe - it's easy to think you need to add more water than you have.

Once pasta is done cooking, reduce to low. Stir slurry and add to pot. Stir pot until slurry is thick (about 1 minute).

Add cheese mix to pot, and turn heat to medium. Stir pot until cheese mix is thick (about 1 minute).

Remove from heat and serve with pepper.
